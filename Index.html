Privacy Policy

Status Saver Pro

Last Updated: May 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Status Saver Pro. This Privacy Policy explains how Status Saver Pro ("we", "our", or "the App") developed by Yuvraj Singh handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Status Saver Pro is designed to help you save and manage WhatsApp statuses, generate stylish fonts, send direct messages, and provide additional utility features. We do not intentionally collect personal information from our users. This policy describes what information may be processed through the App and how third-party services (like Google AdMob and Firebase) integrated into the App collect data.

By using Status Saver Pro, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Status Saver Pro does not intentionally collect, store, or transmit personal information that identifies you as an individual. However, certain information is collected automatically by third-party services integrated into the App to ensure stability and show advertisements:

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number.
  • WhatsApp account credentials or login information.
  • Content of your WhatsApp messages, chats, or personal media.
  • Your contacts list.
  • Biometric data (fingerprint or face data is only used locally on your device for the App Lock feature and is never stored or transmitted).

2.2 Information Automatically Collected (via Third-Party SDKs)

To improve our app and serve advertisements, integrated third-party SDKs (Google Firebase & AdMob) may collect the following data types:

  • Device or other IDs: Non-personal technical information such as device model, OS version, and Advertising IDs (Android Advertising ID) for analytics and advertising purposes.
  • App Activity: Information about how you interact with the App (features used, session duration) to help us understand user behavior.
  • Crash Logs & Diagnostics: Through Firebase Crashlytics, technical information about app crashes and errors is collected to improve app stability and performance.

2.3 Information You Provide

  • Direct Message Feature: When you use the Direct Message feature to send messages without saving contacts, the phone numbers and text are processed locally. This information is NOT stored by us and is passed directly to the official WhatsApp application.
  • Stylish Font Text: Any text you enter into the Font Generator is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected through integrated services is used for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features including saving statuses, generating stylish fonts, and enabling direct messaging.
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and improve overall performance.
  • Crash Reporting: To detect, diagnose, and fix technical issues through Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob, which uses your advertising identifier to show personalized ads.

4. Data Security & Encryption

We take the security of your information seriously and implement appropriate technical measures:

  • Encryption in Transit: Any data transmitted to our third-party service providers (Google AdMob, Firebase) is encrypted in transit using secure protocols (HTTPS).
  • Local Processing: Most app features (status saving, font generation, App Lock) are processed entirely locally on your device.
  • No Central Database: We do not own or maintain any central database storing user information or saved statuses.
  • Biometric Security: Biometric authentication is handled exclusively by your device's secure OS environment.

5. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Status Saver Pro integrates the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:

5.1 AdMob (Google)

We display advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use your advertising identifier to provide personalized advertisements. You can learn more about how Google uses data at: Google Privacy Policy.

5.2 Firebase Services (Google)

We use Firebase Analytics (for usage data), Firebase Crashlytics (for crash reports), and Firebase Cloud Messaging (for push notifications). These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Storage Access Permissions Explained

Status Saver Pro requires storage permissions to function properly. We respect your privacy and only access exactly what is needed:

6.1 Android 11 and Above (Scoped Storage / SAF)

For modern Android devices, we use the Storage Access Framework (SAF). The app will ask you to grant access specifically to the `.Statuses` folder of WhatsApp/WhatsApp Business. We do not request broad access to all your files. We can only read the statuses you explicitly allow us to see.

6.2 Android 10 and Below

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To display WhatsApp statuses saved on your device.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To save your chosen status images and videos to your device's gallery (Pictures/StatusSaver folder).

Important: All saved statuses remain on your device. We do not upload your saved photos or videos to any external server.

7. Children's Privacy

Status Saver Pro is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided information, please contact us.

8. Your Consent & Opt-Out Rights

By using Status Saver Pro, you consent to this Privacy Policy. You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the App at any time, which removes all locally stored app data.
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising by navigating to your device's Settings > Google > Ads > "Opt out of Ads Personalization".
  • Disable App Lock or Push Notifications through the App's settings.
