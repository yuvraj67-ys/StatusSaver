Privacy Policy

Status Saver

Effective Date: January 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Status Saver. This Privacy Policy explains how Status Saver ("we", "our", or "the App") developed by Yuvraj Singh handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Status Saver is designed to help you save and manage WhatsApp statuses, generate stylish fonts, send direct messages, and provide additional utility features. We do not intentionally collect personal information from our users. This policy describes what information may be processed through the App and how third-party services integrated into the App may collect data.

By using Status Saver, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Status Saver does not intentionally collect, store, or transmit personal information that identifies you as an individual. However, certain information may be collected automatically or by third-party services integrated into the App:

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number (unless you voluntarily provide it through the Direct Message feature)
  • WhatsApp account credentials or login information
  • Content of your WhatsApp messages or chats
  • Your contacts list
  • Biometric data (fingerprint or face data is only used locally on your device for App Lock and is never stored or transmitted)

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Non-personal technical information about your device, such as device model, operating system version, screen resolution, and device identifiers (Android Advertising ID) for advertising purposes
  • App Activity and Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including features used, frequency of use, and session duration
  • Crash Reports: Through Firebase Crashlytics, we collect technical information about app crashes and errors to improve app stability and performance
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage statistics to help us understand how users interact with the App and improve user experience
  • Advertising Identifiers: AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and related information to serve personalized advertisements

2.3 Information You Provide

  • Direct Message Feature: When you use the Direct Message feature to send WhatsApp messages without saving contacts, you may enter phone numbers and message text. This information is NOT stored by Status Saver and is only used to facilitate the message sending through WhatsApp
  • Stylish Font Text: Any text you enter into the Font Generator feature is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted or stored by us

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected through Status Saver and its integrated services is used for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features including saving WhatsApp statuses, generating stylish fonts, managing files, and enabling direct messaging
  • App Improvement: To analyze app usage patterns, identify bugs, and improve app performance and user experience
  • Crash Reporting: To detect, diagnose, and fix technical issues and crashes through Firebase Crashlytics
  • Push Notifications: To send you app updates, new feature announcements, or important notifications through Firebase Cloud Messaging (you can disable notifications in your device settings)
  • App Updates: To notify you about available updates and improvements through Firebase In-App Updates
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through AdMob, which may use your advertising identifier to show personalized ads
  • Analytics: To understand user behavior and preferences through Firebase Analytics, helping us make data-driven decisions about app features

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Status Saver implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data processed by the App:

  • Local Processing: Most app features, including status saving, font generation, and app lock, are processed locally on your device
  • No Central Database: We do not maintain a central database of user information or saved statuses
  • Biometric Security: Biometric authentication data (fingerprint/face) is handled exclusively by your device's operating system and is never accessed, stored, or transmitted by Status Saver
  • Secure Connections: Communications with Firebase and AdMob services use encrypted connections
  • Limited Data Collection: By design, we minimize data collection to only what is necessary for app functionality and improvement

However, please note that no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Status Saver integrates several third-party services that may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies:

5.1 AdMob (Google)

Status Saver displays advertisements through Google AdMob, which may show banner ads, interstitial ads, and app open ads. AdMob may collect and use your advertising identifier and related information to provide personalized advertisements. You can learn more about how Google uses data at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 Firebase Services (Google)

We use the following Firebase services:

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data to help us understand app performance and user behavior
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports and diagnostic information to improve app stability
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Enables push notifications for app updates and announcements
  • Firebase In-App Updates: Facilitates app update notifications and installations

Firebase services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.3 Google Play Services

The App uses Google Play Services for various functionalities. Google Play Services may collect information as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

5.4 WhatsApp

Status Saver interacts with WhatsApp to access publicly visible statuses stored on your device and to facilitate direct messaging. We do not access your WhatsApp account, messages, or any information beyond what is stored locally on your device. Your use of WhatsApp is subject to WhatsApp's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand their data practices.

6. Storage Access Permissions

Status Saver requires storage permissions to function properly. Here's why we need these permissions and how we use them:

6.1 READ Storage Permission

  • To access and display WhatsApp statuses saved on your device
  • To show your saved statuses in the built-in gallery viewer
  • To read media files for status saving and management

6.2 WRITE Storage Permission

  • To save WhatsApp status images, videos, and GIFs to your device
  • To organize and manage saved statuses in dedicated folders
  • To enable file management features for saved content

Important: Status Saver only accesses WhatsApp status folders and the folders where saved statuses are stored. We do not access other personal files, documents, or folders on your device. All saved statuses remain on your device and under your control.

7. Biometric Permission

Status Saver offers an App Lock feature that uses biometric authentication (fingerprint or face unlock) to protect your privacy:

  • Purpose: The biometric permission is used exclusively to enable the App Lock feature, which prevents unauthorized access to the App
  • Local Processing: All biometric authentication is handled by your device's secure operating system. Status Saver does not access, store, or transmit your biometric data
  • Optional Feature: Using the App Lock feature is completely optional. You can use Status Saver without enabling this feature
  • Your Control: You can enable or disable App Lock at any time within the App settings

Your biometric data remains secure on your device and is never shared with us or any third party.

8. Direct Message Feature

The Direct Message feature allows you to send WhatsApp messages to phone numbers without saving them as contacts:

  • No Data Storage: Status Saver does NOT store, save, or transmit the phone numbers or message text you enter in this feature
  • Local Processing: The phone number and message are processed locally on your device only to create a WhatsApp intent
  • Direct to WhatsApp: The information is passed directly to WhatsApp, and the actual message sending is handled by WhatsApp according to their privacy practices
  • No Server Communication: This feature does not communicate with any server or external service operated by us

We recommend that you do not share sensitive personal information through this or any messaging feature.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us immediately at the email address provided below, and we will take appropriate steps to address the situation.

Parents and guardians should supervise their children's use of mobile applications and ensure they understand appropriate online behavior and privacy practices.
